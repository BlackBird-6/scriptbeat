INT. COFFEE SHOP - DAY

John sits at the table, staring at his lukewarm coffee. Sarah bursts through the door, looking frantic.

SARAH
(Breathless)
They know!

JOHN
Keep your voice down. Who knows?

SARAH
Everyone! The whole plan is compromised.

John slowly stands up, knocking his chair back. 

JOHN
We need to leave. Now.

EXT. ALLEYWAY - CONTINUOUS

They sprint down the narrow alley. Footsteps echo behind them. The tension rises as they reach a dead end.

SARAH
What now?

John turns around, drawing his weapon.

JOHN
We fight.
